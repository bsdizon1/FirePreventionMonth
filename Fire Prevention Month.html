About Fire Prevention Month

The Fire Prevention Month in the Philippines is March.

March is Fire Prevention Month in the Philippines, making it an ideal opportunity to discuss and raise awareness about the causes, hazards, and dangers of fire. The month of March was designated as "Fire Prevention Month" or "Burn Prevention Month" by Proclamation No. 115-A, signed by President Ferdinand E. Marcos, and Proclamation No. 360 in 1986. According to the proposal, the fire prevention month campaign encourages "safety consciousness" among our people every day of the year as a positive preventive approach to a problem that may be remedied by increased caution, vigilance, sobriety, common sense, and respect for the law.
